Welcome to Algopanda School


Algopanda School, Dehri has been promoted by algopanda Educational & Welfare Society, which includes qualified Engineers with sound technical background and experience in various leading industries and academic institutions, with a view to meet present industrial and technological need of the nation thus serving society through education and development.

algopanda Private School, Dehri has been setup with the sole objective of imparting vocational training to aspiring technicians offering an environment where young minds can grow to their full potential and become disciplined and skilled Technicians. The institutes is also striving for reducing unemployment among educated youth by equipping them with suitable skills for employment and satisfy the requirements of Industries both for manufacturing and service sectors. Quality Training and 100% Placement support has been key factors in making algopanda Private School as the Best School in Bihar.

The institute has been structured with the sole objective of Education and Industrial Training in various Trades required in diversify Industrial Sectors – Manufacturing, Power, Service, Textiles, Automotive, Chemical, Fertilizer, Paper, Refrigeration & Air CondSchooloning etc. to the aspiring technicians offering an environment where they can grow to their full potential and become disciplined and skilled Technicians. The institutes is putting in it’s hundred percent for minimizing unemployment among educated youth by enabling them with suitable Trade skills and Employ-ability for corporate requirement in all Industrial sectors meeting the needs of latest global technology.
