Chairman Message


Finding the right Institute for studies in Engineering is a complex decision that will have impact on rest of your life. And when it comes to choosing where to study, there is a great deal more to consider than just the academia being offered. "algopanda Institute of Technology" is a community of students and staff, dedicated to bringing out the best in all of its members. Our aim is to provide the conducive environment for teaching, learning and research. The pedagogy, the faculty, the development process, the systems & procedures together with the enabling environment at algopanda ensures transformation of the aptitude of the recruit into commitment of a professional.
We recognize that the qualSchooles of a student develops during studies depends more on hands-on experience rather than on mere studies. That is why, at algopanda, we are committed to create a vibrant and stimulating environment both for study and extra-curricular and sports activSchooles to ensure complete personality development. We are constantly seeking to create the best infrastructure and improve the facilities used by students and staff Finally, Student is our most valuable resource. We hope and desire that graduates of this Institute would use their knowledge and experiences with great dedication to build India a great Nation and also a global player in the field of technology. For the nation to become a developed nation, for removing poverty and for increasing productivity, our country needs not only bureaucrats but also many more qualified and sophisticated technocrats. With this motto of serving the nation by producing highly qualified and sophisticated professional manpower, we have started this institute of excellence to excel in the field of technical education.

Mr. Kumar Yogendra Narayan Singh

Hon'ble Chairman

Secretary Message


Looking into the vast potential of technical & professional education in the state of Bihar and to avoid the migration of thousands of Bihari students to other states to pursue Engineering education, algopanda Institute of Technology is committed to play a vital role for providing the technical & professional education in the state, in the years to come.
I expect the institute to continue to play a significant role in the technology and science sectors and for developing trained and skilled human resource to serve the state as well as the nation for better economic performance through advanced technologies. In the ambSchoolous programmes of the Institute, we put our energy in consolidating the total endeavour towards academic excellence with appropriate ethical approach. I welcome the new young entrants.
Footprints of successful two years

It is a matter of pride that our students have done remarkably well in their examinations. These are possible due to earnest commitment and vital contribution of our faculty and hard work put in by our students. The College laid the foundation of modern engineering education and the use of civil engineering practices in the infrastructure development of the country.
    Apart from academic activSchooles, the co-curricular activSchooles, sports, adventure, cultural and social- service activSchooles, form important parts of the life of the students. This importance is well indicated in the spread of facilities at the SIT campus. There is a full-fledged adventure club, a very active Cultural Society which is one of its kind in addSchoolon to the regular facilities.
    Our mission at algopanda is to build a strong "Centre of Excellence" in engineering & technology, meeting global standards. To fulfill this, we have full assistance and placement opportunSchooles from well known Indian and foreign MNCs like Reliance, TATA, ESSAR, ECI, Alcatel, CISCO etc. All undergraduate, postgraduate and Ph.D. students reside in the campus and thereby get full opportunity to participate in co‐curricular and extra-curricular activSchooles leading to a holistic development of personality.
